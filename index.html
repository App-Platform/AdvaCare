About Us

Located in the heart of Bloomington, the leading health professionals in wellness, car accident injuries, injuries on the job, acute injuries, joint pain, and much more. AdvaCare Clinics are dedicated to helping you achieve your wellness objectives - combining skills and expertise that span the entire wellness spectrum. As a patient at AdvaCare, you will have access to an integrated environment combining medical care, chiropractic, and physical therapy.

AdvaCare Clinic has over 85 years combined medical treatment experience, trusted to provide you the care you need to get your health back. Progressive solutions with old-fashioned care; now combining physical therapy, medical, chiropractic, acupuncture, laser, preventive care, wellness and massage in a synergistic approach to healthcare.

AdvaCare is an Outpatient Rehabilitation Facility Specializing in Chronic and Acute Pain Treatments, combining physical therapy, medical, chiropractic, acupuncture, laser, preventive care, wellness and massage in a synergistic approach to healthcare.

AdvaCare is multidisciplinary with each discipline bringing a unique knowledge and skill set to the rehabilitation process. The patient and his family members are vital members of the rehabilitation team and are integrated early into the rehabilitation process assisting with goal setting and other essential management strategies.

In addition to involving patients and family members in discussions about the goals of the rehabilitation program, the staff extends a wide variety of services to family members as well as patients, including psychological counseling and educational programs that can address all questions about rehabilitation.

Our dedication to providing expert and compassionate care can make an important difference in a patient's rehabilitation and change their outlook on the future. AdvaCare shows patients how they can achieve and maintain a level of improved functioning they may have not thought possible.

The care that you will receive is tailored to meet your needs and goals. You will feel confident knowing that you are getting real solutions, not just quick fixes.

In order to better serve you, the Advanced Chiropractic team incorporates a holistic approach to your health care needs. We will assess your needs structurally, nutritionally, and emotionally.

All this is done in a safe and peaceful environment where you can relax and heal.

First 10

If you are in a car accident here is what to do in the...

First Ten Minutes

First 10 Minutes after a car accident:

  • Stay calm - assess passengers
  • Call 9-1-1 immediately if injuries or severe vehicle damage
  • Move out of traffic IF it's safe
  • Turn hazard lights on
  • Call police - stay until they arrive
  • Be polite - don't admit fault, give only facts to the police
  • Take pictures if safe (use cell camera), take notes
  • Exchange: name, address, phone, license plate, driver's license, insurance
  • Ask witnesses for names, phone numbers, addresses

First Ten Hours

First 10 hours after a car accident:

  • Secure vehicle - remove valuables
  • See AdvaCare Clinics ASAP, even if injuries seem minor
  • Record weather, time, location, statements made, etc.
  • File accident report as required by state law
  • Contact insurance agent
  • Revisit accident scene - photograph anything important
  • Photograph visible injuries
  • Obtain police report when available
  • Get repair estimates for your car
  • Contact employer if you can't work

First Ten Days

First 10 days after a car accident:

  • See AdvaCare Clinics ASAP, even if injuries seem minor, soft tissue injuries can go unnoticed for months, after 12 weeks permanent damage can set in causing chronic pain
  • Get a spinal stress test to check for joint, ligament, or soft tissue injury, even with lack of pain or severe pain

Contact an Attorney

When to contact an attorney:

  • If injured and/or extensive vehicle damage
  • Before talking to another driver's insurance company
  • If you are being pressured into a quick settlement
  • Before the time period for filing a claim expires
